Project Worked

The name of our project is the " Vendor-to-Retailer Fruit and Vegetable Selling App." Our application is designed to facilitate retailers in ordering fresh fruits and vegetables, which will be promptly delivered to their stores. The primary goal of our app is to minimize wastage of perishable goods in retail shops, a challenge often caused by fluctuations in customer demand. Through our app, retailers can conveniently place orders for products precisely when they are in demand, helping them manage their inventory more effectively and reduce unnecessary losses.
